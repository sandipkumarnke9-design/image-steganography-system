Technique Information

LSB (Least Significant Bit)

Modifies the least significant bits of pixel values to embed data. Provides high capacity with minimal visual impact. Best for uncompressed images.

DCT (Discrete Cosine Transform)

Embeds data in frequency domain coefficients. More robust against image modifications and compression. Better for images that may be compressed.
